In my free time(which seems to be steadily decreasing), I like to work out solutions to the problems on project-euler. This is just where I put my solutions. Most of the problems will either be done in Java or in Python, depending on my mood for the day. I may also start to pick up other languages(that are a lot faster to run), but currently Java and Python are the easiest languages to just work out the logic in so they're the ones I will be using. 

All past problems will be located in the [archive](https://li-michelle.github.io/tag/archive/). The current viewable problem is the one that I am working on and since Project Euler has a policy of not revealing answers past the first 100 problems, I'll regretably have to put only my initial reactions after the first 100.
